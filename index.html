Oral Health Tips

Prevent Before You Need to Cure

Simple daily habits that protect your smile for life.

Brush Twice Daily

Brush for two minutes each morning and night with a soft-bristled brush and fluoride toothpaste to remove plaque and protect enamel.

Daily Habit

Floss Every Day

Flossing removes plaque and food particles from between teeth that your toothbrush can't reach — preventing cavities and gum disease.

Daily Habit

Limit Sugary Foods

Sugar feeds the bacteria that produce enamel-eroding acids. Limit sweets, sodas, and sticky foods — especially between meals.

Diet Tip

Visit Every 6 Months

Regular check-ups catch problems early — before they become painful or expensive. A professional clean every 6 months is essential.

Preventive Care

Drink More Water

Water washes away food particles, keeps your mouth moist, and helps neutralise acids. Fluoridated water also strengthens enamel.

Hydration

Replace Toothbrush Every 3 Months

Frayed bristles lose their cleaning effectiveness and harbour bacteria. Replace your brush every 3 months for optimal results.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you want to know before visiting us — answered clearly and honestly.

Do you treat children?+
Yes — we provide complete pediatric dental care for children of all ages, in a friendly, anxiety-free environment designed to make kids feel comfortable and safe.
How do I book an appointment?+
You can book by calling or WhatsApp, or by filling the appointment form on this page. We will confirm within a few hours and send you a reminder before your visit.
Are your treatments painful?+
We use advanced local anaesthesia and gentle techniques to ensure all our procedures are as comfortable as possible. Most patients are surprised by how pain-free modern dentistry is.
Do you accept dental insurance?+
We work with several dental insurance providers and help you maximise your benefits. Please call us with your insurance details and we will confirm coverage before your appointment.
How long does a routine checkup take?+
A routine checkup including examination and professional cleaning usually takes 45 minutes to an hour. We will let you know if anything additional is needed after the exam.
What are your clinic hours?+
We are open Monday to Saturday, 9 AM to 7 PM. We also offer early morning slots on select days — call us to check availability for your preferred time.
Can I get Invisalign as an adult?+
Absolutely. Invisalign is highly effective for adults and a growing number of our patients choose it for its discreet appearance. We offer free Invisalign consultations.
How do I know if I need a root canal?+
Common signs include severe toothache, sensitivity to hot and cold that lingers, swelling near the tooth, or tooth discolouration. Come in for an examination and we will diagnose accurately.
